Happy 1st Anniversary, my love. ❤️

Wala ko katuo nga one year na diay ta sukad nagsugod ta haha. So much has happened in these past twelve months, and every single moment with you has become a memory that I will always treasure.

Thank you for staying by my side through everything — the happy days, the stressful moments, the little misunderstandings. Despite everything, you chose to stay, to understand me, and to love me. I honestly don't think I say this enough, but I appreciate you more than words can ever express.

Kabalo ka, ever since na nagka-close ta, I've realized that you've been on my mind all the time. A simple conversation with you, your laugh, your random updates, your "good morningg" and "good nightt" messages — they became the best parts of my day. You became my comfort, my peace, and my favorite person.

Usahay dili ko maayo mo-express sa akong gibati. Dili ko pirmi kabalo unsaon pag-ingon sa sakto nga mga words, pero gusto nako nga makahibalo ka nga tinuod kaayo akong gugma para nimo. Every "I love you" I tell you comes from the deepest part of my heart.

Thank you for believing in me even when I doubt myself. Thank you for encouraging me to become a better person. Because of you, I learned to love more deeply, to be more patient, and to appreciate the little things in life.

I'm sorry for the times I've hurt you, whether intentionally or unintentionally. I know I'm not perfect, and I still have so much to learn. But one thing I can promise is that I will always choose you. I will continue working on myself because you deserve someone who gives you the best version of himself.

You are beautiful not just because of your smile or your eyes, but because of your heart. The way you care, the way you love, the way you make people around you feel valued... that's what makes you truly amazing. I hope you never forget how special you are.

Salamat kaayo sa tanan, baby. Salamat sa imong pasensya, sa imong pagsabot, sa imong paghigugma bisan sa akong mga flaws. Ikaw ang usa sa pinakamaayong butang nga nahitabo sa akong kinabuhi, ug wala gyud ko nagmahay nga ikaw akong gihigugma.

By celebrating our first anniversary, I don't just celebrate the memories we've made — I celebrate the amazing person I'm lucky enough to call mine, hehe. I know there will still be challenges ahead, but kung naa ka, kaya ra nato ni ma overcome bisan unsa, bahala na basta together ta.

I can't promise that every day will be perfect. I can't promise we will never argue or make mistakes. But I can promise that I will never stop choosing us. I will keep loving you, supporting you, respecting you, and reminding you every day how important you are to me.

My dream isn't just to spend another year with you. I want many more anniversaries. I want more adventures, more laughter, more late-night conversations, more random dates, more achievements together, and more memories that we'll look back on years from now.

No matter where life takes us, I hope you'll continue holding my hand. Because as long as you're beside me, I know I'll always be home.

Happy 1st Anniversary, my love.

Thank you for making this past year the happiest chapter of my life. Here's to our first anniversary, and to all the years that are still waiting for us.

I love you more than words can ever explain.
